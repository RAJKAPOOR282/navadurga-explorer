Navratri

9-day festival dedicated to Maa Durga, celebrated with prayers, fasting, and dance.

Durga Puja

Mainly celebrated in Bengal, Odisha, and Assam. Elaborate pandals and rituals are done.

Dussehra

Marks the victory of Maa Durga (or Lord Rama) over evil forces. Celebrated with Ramlila and effigies of Ravana.
